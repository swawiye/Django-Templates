Our Mission

We believe in freedom of every human being by upholiding their fundamental rights and freedoms. At organa we do this by ensuring that each donor and recipient is catered to appropriately during the organ donation process by storing their records.

What 'Organa' mean? Oragan means instrument in Latin. The meaning of an organ in Latin is derived from it. Organs, just like instruments of work, are essential in the proper function of the human body.
